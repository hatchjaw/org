* Appearance

#+begin_src emacs-lisp
(setq default-frame-alist '(
#+end_src

** Frame size

Fullscreen as standard.

#+begin_src emacs-lisp
  (fullscreen . maximized)
#+end_src

** Background colour

Set the default background colour to match ~bg1~ from
the dark version of =spacemacs-theme=.
https://github.com/nashamri/spacemacs-theme/blob/6c74684c4d55713c8359bedf1936e429918a8c33/spacemacs-theme.el#L108

#+begin_src emacs-lisp
  (background-color . "#292b2e")
#+end_src

** Default frame elements

#+begin_src emacs-lisp
  (vertical-scroll-bars . nil)
  (menu-bar-lines . 0)
  (tool-bar-lines . 0)))
#+end_src

** UI Settings

Don't show the menu, tool, or scroll bars.

#+begin_src emacs-lisp
(menu-bar-mode -1)
(tool-bar-mode -1)
(scroll-bar-mode -1)
#+end_src
