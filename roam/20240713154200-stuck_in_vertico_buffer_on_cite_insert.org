#+title: Stuck in vertico buffer on cite insert

So, you've entered =C-c C-x @= in a valiant attempt to add a citation
to your org document. Now you find that "empty input exits", except
you can't provide an empty input because vertico has helpfully listed
all of your bibliograhy entries. Solution: hit =M-Ret=.
