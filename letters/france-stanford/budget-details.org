As an accompaniment to my application for the France-Stanford
Institute's Visiting Student Researcher fellowship, please consider
this estimate of expenses anticipated during my visit. Estimates are
concerned with the proposed three-month period of 1 April to 30
June 2026. Please consult the table below for details.

#+attr_latex: :booktabs t :center t :align lrrr :float nil
|--------------------------------+------------+-------------------+---------------|
| *Item*                         | *Quantity* | *Unit cost (USD)* | *Total (USD)* |
|--------------------------------+------------+-------------------+---------------|
| Airfare                        |          2 |             1,000 | 2,000         |
| Housing (monthly)              |          3 |             1,250 | 3,750         |
| Food (monthly)                 |          3 |               500 | 1,500         |
| Transportation (monthly)       |          3 |               100 | 300           |
| Human Subject Costs            |         29 |                50 | 1,450         |
|--------------------------------+------------+-------------------+---------------|
| *Total Research Funding*       |            |                   | *9,000*       |
|--------------------------------+------------+-------------------+---------------|
| VSR Tuition Fee (three months) |            |                   | 4,000         |
|--------------------------------+------------+-------------------+---------------|
| *Grand Total*                  |            |                   | *13,000*      |
|--------------------------------+------------+-------------------+---------------|

/Airfare/ refers to a return fare from Paris Charles de Gaulle to San
Francisco, and includes train relay between Paris and my residence in
Lyon, France. /Human Subject Costs/ represent compensation for
experimental participants in the evaluation of a distributed spatial
audio system. Compensation of this sort will be handled in accordance
with appropriate ethical guidelines.[fn:1]

If you have any queries about the information presented here, please
do not hesitate to contact me.
