#+begin_quote
The SOUNDS European Training Network (ETN) revolves around a new and
promising paradigm coined as Service-Oriented, Ubiquitous,
Network-Driven Sound.
#+end_quote

Toon van Waterschoot gave a [[file:~/org/journal.org::*IS^{2}: Keynote: Toon van Waterschoot][keynote]] on this project at [[denote:20241013T170234][IS2]] in
Erlangen.

* About

- Ubiquity of mobile/wearable devices capable of
  capturing/processing/reproducing sound.
- Intention to exploit network-enabled cooperation between devices.
- IoT opportunities; ad-hoc networks.
- Aim to create a software layer in which audio devices cooperate on
  top of IoT connectivity.
- Next-gen audio tech capable of hearing assistance, immersive audio,
  voice control, etc.
- Exchange of signals and control data, spatial analysis/processing.
- /Audio as a Service/

* Research objectives

- Distributed Acoustic Array Signal Processing
- Network-Driven Audio Signal Processing
- Acoustic Awareness
