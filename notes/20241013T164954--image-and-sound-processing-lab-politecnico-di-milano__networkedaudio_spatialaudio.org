I was introduced to [[https://www.deib.polimi.it/ita/personale/dettagli/841564][Alessandro Ilic Mezza]] and [[https://www.deib.polimi.it/ita/personale/dettagli/936408][Alberto Bernardini]] from
ISPL (by Christina Rottondi) at [[denote:20241013T170234][IS2]] in Erlangen. They were quite
instrumental in the conference, running the packet loss concealment
challenge, Alessandro (interest in ML, "neural audio codecs", etc.)
chairing the first paper session.

Alberto knows Romain, and it seems there's some overlap in research
interest, e.g. the ISPL thesis [[https://ispl.deib.polimi.it/thesis/][proposal]], /"Spatial audio in networked
music performance applications."/

Worth bearing these guys and their work in mind for future
collaboration, perhaps, and certainly for perusing recent
publications.
