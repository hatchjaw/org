Met with Christof Ressi of the Audio Over OSC project back in
June. [[file:~/org/journal.org::*Meeting: Christof Ressi][Notes from the meeting]] are in my journal.

Could be a useful platform on which to build an audio server at some
point. Compare with [[denote:20241012T170404][Corelink/Holodeck]]. [[https://sonobus.net/][Sonobus]] uses AOO under the
hood, apparently ([[https://github.com/sonosaurus/sonobus?tab=readme-ov-file#license-and-3rd-party-software][yep]]).
